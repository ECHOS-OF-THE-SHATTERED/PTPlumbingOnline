01

Data Collection & Intake

PT Plumbing Inc. (hereinafter "The Company") collects specific digital and physical data points to facilitate emergency dispatch and plumbing repair services. This data collection occurs at the "Point of Contact" (POC) via our digital forms, telephonic uplink, or on-site estimation tablets.

1.1 Identifiable Information

We collect "Personally Identifiable Information" (PII) strictly for the purpose of service execution. This includes:

  • Full Legal Name (for billing and warranty registration).
  • Physical Service Address (for GPS routing of tactical units).
  • Telephonic Contact Digits (for dispatch notifications).
  • Email Protocols (for digital invoicing and estimates).
  • Payment Gateway Tokens (processed via encrypted third-party merchant services; we do not store raw credit card integers).

1.2 Technical Telemetry

When you access this digital node (website), our servers automatically log standard telemetry data including IP addresses, browser user-agents, and referral paths. This data is used solely for "SmartFirewall" threat detection and optimization of our digital infrastructure.

02

Usage Protocols

Data gathered by The Company is utilized under strict operational parameters. We do not engage in data mining or behavioral surplus extraction.

2.1 Operational Dispatch

Your address and contact status are transmitted to the specific Field Technician assigned to your sector via a secure, encrypted mobile app. Once the service call is closed, the technician loses access to your data on their mobile device.

2.2 Communication Channels

By submitting a dispatch request, you authorize The Company to contact you via:

  • SMS / Text: For "En Route" notifications and technician ID verification photos.
  • Voice: For urgent triage and confirmation of appointment windows.
  • Email: For sending PDF invoices, warranty certificates, and annual maintenance reminders.
03

Digital Security & Encryption

We employ enterprise-grade security architecture to protect your digital footprint. Our systems are audited by Dye Security protocols to ensure zero-day resilience.

SSL / TLS 1.3

All data in transit is encrypted using the latest Transport Layer Security protocols.

AES-256 Storage

Data at rest in our archives is encrypted with military-grade 256-bit keys.

Access Control

Only Level-4 administrative staff have full access to client historical databases.

04

Third Party Interfacing

We are a plumbing company, not a data broker. We have a strict "No Sell" policy regarding your information.

4.1 Vendor Isolation

We only share necessary data with the following essential operational partners:

  • Merchant Processors: To facilitate credit card transactions.
  • Government Entities: Only when required for pulling city permits for your specific project.
  • Manufacturer Warranty Depts: To register your specific equipment (e.g., Navien, Rinnai) for warranty coverage.

We do not share your data with marketing agencies, lead generators, or other trade companies.

05

Client Rights & Control

You retain full sovereignty over your data profile. In accordance with CCPA and general privacy standards, you maintain the following rights:

Right to Deletion (The "Purge" Protocol) +

You may request the permanent deletion of your digital profile from our active marketing lists. Note: Transactional data required for tax and warranty purposes must be retained by law for a minimum of 7 years.

Right to Inspection +

You may request a copy of all data The Company holds regarding your profile. This "Data Packet" will be delivered via secure email within 30 days of a verified request.

Opt-Out Mechanisms +

Every marketing email contains a "Unsubscribe" link. You may also reply "STOP" to any SMS communication to immediately cease digital text correspondence.
