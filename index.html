Common Queries

Field data regarding frequent client concerns.

How fast is your emergency response time?

For critical failures (uncontrolled leaks, sewage backup), our dispatch goal is under 60 minutes. We utilize GPS fleet tracking to route the nearest tactical unit to your coordinates immediately.

Do you offer flat-rate pricing?

Yes. We do not charge by the hour. We charge by the job code. You will receive a precise, down-to-the-penny price before any work begins. The price does not change even if the job takes longer than expected.

Is your work warrantied?

Absolutely. Most standard repairs carry a 2-year warranty. Main line replacements and re-pipes carry a lifetime workmanship warranty. We use materials designed to last 50+ years.
