Electronic Leak Detection

Water leaks under a concrete foundation (Slab Leaks) can cause catastrophic structural damage if left untreated. We do not use "guess-and-check" demolition. We utilize sensitive acoustic ground microphones, thermal imaging cameras, and tracer gas to pinpoint the exact location of the pipe failure within inches.

THE STRATEGY: Minimize destruction. Maximize precision. Once located, we offer direct spot repair, re-routing of the line, or complete epoxy pipe restoration.

Stop paying to heat water you aren't using. Tankless systems provide infinite hot water on demand. We are factory-certified installers for Navien and Rinnai. Hydro-Jetting & Descaling

Mechanical snakes ("rooters") only poke a hole in the blockage. Hydro-Jetting uses high-pressure water (4000 PSI) to scrub the internal diameter of the pipe, removing years of grease, scale, and root intrusion. It is the closest thing to a new pipe without excavation.

CAMERA VERIFICATION: Every cleaning service includes a before-and-after digital recording of your sewer main line to verify flow restoration.
