CHRONOLOGICAL DATABASE

Operation Timeline

From a single van to a fleet of mobile workshops.

2010

Initialization

PT Plumbing is established by Phillip Thomas after witnessing the dishonest sales tactics of major franchises. Starting with one used truck and a credit card for tools, the mission was simple: honest engineering.

2014

The Digital Shift

We abandoned paper invoicing years before the industry standard. We implemented iPad-based diagnostic reports, allowing homeowners to see photos and videos of their plumbing issues in real-time.

2018

Trenchless Adoption

Acquired "The Mole" directional drilling units and Epoxy Relining systems. We stopped destroying driveways to fix pipes, offering surgical, no-dig solutions.

2025

Current Era

Now a team of 12 licensed mechanics. Fully operational training center for apprentices. Integrating AI-assisted scheduling to minimize client wait times to under 60 minutes.

Honor Protocol

We will never sell you something you do not need. If a repair is possible and safe, we will offer it before suggesting a replacement.

No Sub-Contractors

Everyone who enters your home is a W-2 employee, background checked, drug tested, and personally trained by Phillip. We do not farm out work.

Total Transparency

Price before work. You sign a tablet authorizing the exact dollar amount. There are no surprise bills, no "we found something else" hidden fees.
