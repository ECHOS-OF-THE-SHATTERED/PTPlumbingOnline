01

Definitions & Acceptance

This Agreement is made between PT Plumbing Inc. (hereinafter referred to as "Contractor", "We", "Us") and the property owner or authorized agent (hereinafter referred to as "Client", "Customer", "You").

1.1 Acceptance of Terms

Dispatching a technician, signing an estimate (digital or physical), or allowing work to commence constitutes full acceptance of these Terms of Service. These terms supersede any verbal agreements unless documented in writing by an officer of PT Plumbing Inc.

02

Scope of Work & Estimates

We pride ourselves on transparent pricing, but plumbing infrastructure is often hidden behind walls or underground.

2.1 Estimates vs. Bids

Estimates provided before visual inspection are non-binding ranges. A binding "Bid" or "Quote" is only provided after a technician has physically inspected the system.

2.2 Unforeseen Conditions

Our quotes cover the specific scope listed. If, during the course of work, we encounter:

  • Hidden mold, asbestos, or lead paint.
  • Illegal or non-code compliant prior workmanship.
  • Rotted framing or structural damage.
  • Collapsed pipes previously inaccessible to cameras.

Work will cease immediately, and a "Change Order" will be presented. We are not responsible for pre-existing illegal conditions.

03

Payment Protocols

We are a service company, not a bank. Prompt payment ensures we can continue to provide emergency services to the community.

3.1 Due Upon Completion

Payment is due in full immediately upon the substantial completion of the scope of work. We accept Credit Cards, Checks, and Cash.

3.2 Deposits

Any project exceeding $1,000.00 requires a 50% deposit to schedule and procure materials. This deposit is non-refundable once materials have been ordered.

3.3 Late Fees

Invoices not paid within 7 days of completion will accrue a late fee of 1.5% per month (18% APR) or the maximum allowed by law. Mechanics Liens will be filed on properties with unpaid balances exceeding 30 days.

04

Warranty Policy

We stand behind our craftsmanship. However, we cannot warranty what we do not touch.

4.1 Standard Labor Warranty

2-Year Warranty on labor for all new installations and repairs, excluding drain cleaning. If our workmanship fails, we fix it for free.

4.2 Drain Cleaning Exclusion

Drain cleaning / Hydro-Jetting is a maintenance task, not a repair. We warranty that the line is flowing upon our departure. We DO NOT warranty against future blockages caused by roots, broken pipes, or user abuse (wipes, grease, foreign objects) unless the pipe was replaced by us.

4.3 Parts Warranty

Parts are covered by the manufacturer's warranty. We will facilitate the exchange of defective parts, but labor charges may apply after the initial 1-year period depending on the specific product.

05

Site Access & Safety

To perform our duties safely and efficiently, the Client must provide a suitable workspace.

5.1 Clear Access

Client agrees to remove personal property (furniture, boxes, etc.) from the work area (under sinks, around water heaters) prior to arrival. Contractor is not liable for damage to personal property left in the work zone.

5.2 Pet & Minor Safety

Client agrees to secure all pets and keep minors away from the work zone. Our tools are sharp, hot, and heavy. We are not responsible for the safety of unsupervised pets or children.

06

Limitation of Liability

Water is a destructive force. While we take every precaution, certain risks are inherent to plumbing repair.

6.1 Pre-Existing Damage

Contractor is not liable for pre-existing water damage, mold, or rot discovered during the repair.

6.2 Drywall & Landscaping

Unless specifically line-itemed in the quote, our price covers PLUMBING ONLY. We do not patch drywall, paint, replace tile, or replant landscaping disturbed during excavation. We will backfill trenches to "rough grade" only.

6.3 Pressure Testing

During diagnostics, we may need to pressure test systems. Old, corroded pipes may fail during this standard test. Contractor is not liable for leaks that appear in old systems during standard testing procedures; this is considered a failure of the existing material, not negligence.

07

Cancellation & Rescheduling

Time is our most valuable inventory.

7.1 Notification

Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations.

7.2 Dispatch Fee

If a technician arrives at the property and is unable to enter, or the client cancels at the door, a standard Dispatch Fee ($89.00) will be charged to cover fuel and travel time.
