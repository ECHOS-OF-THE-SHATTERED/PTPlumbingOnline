TECHNOLOGY ANALYSIS

Tank vs. Tankless

Understanding the architectural differences in water heating.

Standard Tank

LEGACY TECH

Heats 40-50 gallons 24/7, regardless of use. Prone to sediment buildup and leaks after 8-10 years.

  • High Standby Energy Loss
  • Limited Hot Water Supply
  • Large Footprint (Garage/Closet)
  • Lower Upfront Cost

Tankless System

MODERN STANDARD

Heats water instantly on demand. Wall-mounted design frees up space. Stainless steel heat exchangers resist corrosion.

  • Endless Hot Water
  • 30-40% Energy Savings
  • 20+ Year Lifespan
  • Compact Wall Mount
ENGINEERING SPECS

Installation Protocol

Installing a tankless unit is not a simple swap. It requires precise gas load calculations and venting adjustments. We adhere to strict manufacturer specifications.

01 // GAS LINE SIZING

Tankless units require up to 199,000 BTU. We verify your gas meter and pipe diameter can support the load without starving other appliances.

02 // VENTING OPTIMIZATION

We install dedicated PVC or Polypropylene exhaust venting to safely expel combustion gases, adhering to all Casa Grande building codes.

03 // SERVICE VALVES

Every installation includes isolation service valves, allowing for easy annual descaling and maintenance without cutting pipes.

Hard Water Warning

Casa Grande has extremely hard water. Without annual flushing (descaling), mineral deposits will destroy your heat exchanger within 3-5 years.

We offer a flat-rate annual flush service to protect your investment.
