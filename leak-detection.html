Identify The Breach

If you observe these anomalies, your system integrity is compromised.

Hot Spots on Floor

A specific area of your floor feels warm to the touch. This indicates a hot water line fracture in the slab.

Spinning Meter

If your water meter's leak indicator triangle is spinning while all fixtures are off, you have a mainline breach.

Cracked Foundation

Water saturation causes soil expansion (heaving), leading to hairline cracks in walls or baseboards.

THE EQUIPMENT

Goldak & FLIR Technology

We do not guess. We verify. Our technicians are equipped with the industry standard in non-destructive testing equipment.

01 // ACOUSTIC SONAR

High-sensitivity ground microphones amplify the sound of escaping water (hissing) through concrete, filtering out ambient noise.

02 // THERMAL IMAGING

Infrared cameras detect temperature differentials of less than 0.1°F, revealing the path of hot water lines instantly.

03 // TRACER GAS

For difficult leaks, we inject a harmless hydrogen-nitrogen mix into the line and use a "sniffer" to detect gas escaping through the floor.

RESOLUTION PROTOCOLS

Repair Strategies

Once located, we present three distinct tactical options.

OPTION A

Spot Repair

We open a small 2x2 access point in the floor, cut out the damaged section, and repair it. Best for newer homes with isolated damage.

  • Lowest Cost
  • Invasive to Flooring
OPTION B

Re-Route

We abandon the failing line under the floor and run a brand new PEX line through the walls or attic. No concrete is broken.

  • Non-Invasive
  • Upgrades Piping
OPTION C

Epoxy Lining

We clean the pipe internally and coat it with a structural epoxy resin, sealing the leak from the inside out.

  • Zero Destruction
  • Higher Cost

Diagnostic FAQ

Does homeowners insurance cover slab leaks?

Typically, yes. Most policies cover the "tear-out and replacement" of the floor to access the pipe, which is the expensive part. They often do not cover the $200 pipe repair itself. We provide detailed reports specifically for insurance claims.

How accurate is the detection?

Extremely accurate. Our specialized acoustic equipment allows us to pinpoint the leak within a 1-2 foot radius, ensuring we don't dig up your entire living room.

What is the cost of detection?

Our standard electronic leak detection fee is $289.00. However, if you choose us to perform the repair or re-route, we credit a portion of this fee back to the final project cost.
